Research Framework

Our process combines business-quality assessment, financial statement analysis, valuation discipline, and clearly stated investment conclusions.

Business Quality

Analysis of industry structure, competitive advantages, pricing power, management quality, and durability of returns on invested capital.

Financial Analysis

Revenue growth, margin structure, free cash flow conversion, balance sheet strength, and reinvestment capacity are evaluated across cycles.

Valuation

Intrinsic value is assessed through comparable companies, precedent transactions, discounted cash flow analysis, and scenario-weighted outcomes.
